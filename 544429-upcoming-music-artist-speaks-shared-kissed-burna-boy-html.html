Why I Kissed Burna Boy- Cynthia Morgan

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, April 14, 2024

The Nigerian dance-hall queen, Cynthia Morgan, has kept her relationship status a secret until recently when rumors linked her with fellow dance-hall singer, Burna Boy.

When asked about their sizzling romance, the music stars denied ever anything to do with each other.

In her reaction to the lingering rumor, the German Juice crooner in a chat with HipTv speaks on why she shared a kiss with Burna Boy.

Cynthia Morgan said “Yes, the kiss between me and Burna boy was real, though we are working together on a project at the moment. The kiss was part of the video we are shooting and yeah, the dude is a talented artiste”.

When further asked about what she said in the past, saying she would not date any artiste in the industry,

Cynthia disclosed that “I have any answer to that but what matters at the end of the day is my happiness and nothing more than that”.

It can be recalled that, Cynthia Morgan made the social media go gaga when she posted photos of herself and the Port Harcourt born star kissing passionately on Instagram. However, the two acts did little to deny the relationship as new confirmation emerged that they are having an affair.

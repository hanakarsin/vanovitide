Fans celebrate late American actress Naya Rivera on her 34th birthday

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Sunday, April 28, 2024

- Fans of late American star, Naya Rivera, took to social media to celebrate what would have been her 34th birthday

- The late actress drowned in a tragic accident last year while she was out boating with her young son

- Online users posted messages and tributes in honour of Rivera's birthday on Tuesday, January 12

The late American actress, Naya Rivera, would have celebrated her 34th birthday today, Tuesday, January 12, probably with fans, friends, and most importantly her handsome son.

The star, mostly known for her role on the popular musical series Glee, tragically drowned in 2020 while on a boating trip with her young son.

Fans decided to honour Naya's special day by posting birthday wishes, video montages, and heartfelt tributes on social media with promises not to ever forget her.

They also shared different photos of the late actress on social media and commemorated the day by wishing her a happy heavenly birthday.

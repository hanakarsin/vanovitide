Nigerian man wants to sell his dog for N1.1m, many react

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Friday, February 2, 2024

- A Nigerian man has given many people things to talk about after showcasing a six-month-old dog he wants to sell for N1.1 million

- Many people asked all manner of questions, as some said they want to know if the pet is a passport to Canada

- Photos of the dog went viral online with both serious and hilarious comments on the significance of dogs

A Nigerian man with the Twitter handle @KidMarleymusic has stirred massive conversation after offering to sell a dog for N1.1m.

He said that the dog is 6 months old. Many people could not just help but wonder why such a pet would be so expensive.

Many people jokingly asked if he wants to sell a house, a dog, or both as they just could not place buying the animal for that much.

Those who understoodd that the showcased dog is a rarity and how costly it could be asked him for his location so they can come check it out.

'You Brought Out The Mother In Me' DJ Cuppy Celebrates Her 'Sons' Birthday With Matching Outfits

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Saturday, March 23, 2024

Nigerian disc jockey and entertainer, Florence Otedola better known as DJ Cuppy has taken to her Instagram handle to celebrate the first birthday of her pet dogs, Dudu and Funfun.

Naija News reports that the billionaire daughter shared photos of herself together with her dogs as they wore matching outfits to celebrate the day on 17th February, 2022.

She captioned the photos; “Happy 1st Birthday to my sons @DuFuPoms You bring some happiness to my hectic life! Although small and fluffy, you radiate unconditional love in such a big way. You’ve brought out the mother in me… I LOVE YOU BOTH so much!”

Meanwhile, DJ Cuppy recently asked out an Ibadan-based man to Valentine celebration.

In a tweet seen by Naija News, the billionaire daughter asked a man with the Twitter handle A$AP Larry to be her 2022 Valentine date.

She acknowledged their destination issues, as she is currently in Oxford and he is in Ibadan, suggesting that they should meet on Twitter on February 14th.

“Dear A$AP Lerry (@_AsiwajuLerry) Will you be my 2022 #Valentine ? I know you’re in Ibadan, and I’m in Oxford but let’s meet on Twitter Space at 7pm on Feb 14th for our first date”

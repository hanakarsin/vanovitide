See Cristiano Ronaldo's First Two Goals In Juventus

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Monday, April 22, 2024

If you have not seen, see. Mark this date in the calendar – 09/16/2018. It was on Sunday that Ronaldo scored the first goals in his Juventus jersey in the victory of ‘vecchia signora’ against Sassuolo.

Juventus defeated Sassuolo 2-1 in Sunday’s 4-2 fixture with two goals from Portuguese international Cristiano Ronaldo.

In Turin, Juventus presented two Portuguese internationals in the starting lineup with Cristiano Ronaldo and João Cancelo in Allegri’s choices. Playing in the home of the Italian champions, Sassuolo sought to create danger but it was the Turin formation to be closer to the goal.

At half-time, the scoreboard registered a null after some scoring opportunities missed by both teams. Cristiano Ronaldo appeared in good plan, but at the end of the first part still had not managed to ‘break the fast’ of goals.

In the second half, Juventus advanced on the scoreboard in the 50 ‘minutes through Cristiano Ronaldo. The Portuguese international took advantage of a second ball in the small area of ​​Sassuolo to open the head active and debut in this way to score with the Juventus jersey.

In the 65 ‘minute, Cristiano Ronaldo returned to inscribe his name in the history of the game with 2-0 to emerge following a rapid counterattack. The Portuguese international received the ball of Emre Can and shot strong and placed for the second goal of the game.

DJ Cuppy Sparks Break-Up Rumours With Fiance, Ryan Taylor

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, February 8, 2024

Nigerian Disc Jockey, Florence Otedola popularly known as DJ Cuppy and British boxer cum fiance, Ryan Taylor has sparked breaking-up rumours months after they got engaged in Abu Dhabi.

Naija News reports that this comes after the fans of the Nigerian DJ speculated all is not well with the relationship between Cuppy and her fiancé, Ryan Taylor.

A quick check, showed that the English YouTuber and prankster is no longer following the Cuppy, coupled with the fact that the incessant tagging and posting of Ryan by Cuppy has also stopped.

In another post on his Instagram story, Ryan hinted about letting go of something that blocks one’s spirituality.

His post reads; “If it’s blocking your spiritual growth. Let it go.”

DJ Cuppy Visits Grandmother, Dishes Advise

Meanwhile, DJ Cuppy recently took to  social media to share a piece of advice to her fans after visiting her maternal grandmother

According to the 30-year-old DJ, visiting her grandmother reminded her of the importance of honouring the previous generations.

She also advised her followers to show respect and appreciation to the elders because it is a bridge that transports one to the future.

She tweeted, “Today I had the privilege of visiting my mum’s mum. Seeing my grandma reminded me of how important it is to honour our previous generations.

“When we show respect and appreciation for those who have come before us, we create a bridge to our past that we can carry with us into the future.”

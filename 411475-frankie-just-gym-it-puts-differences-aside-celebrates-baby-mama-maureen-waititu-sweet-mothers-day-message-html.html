Just like many other people worldwide, nutritionist Frankie Just Gym It took to social media on Sunday, May 6, to commemorate International Mother's Day.

Apart from celebrating his mother and girlfriend Corazon Kwamboka, the fitness coach put differences aside and acknowledged his baby mama Maureen Waititu.

About six months ago, Frankie and Wiatitu traded barbs on social media with the latter accusing the former of not providing for his kids and kicking her out of his mother's house.

However, taking to Instagram, the fitness trainer officially known as Francis Kiarie, reminisced over their good old days, insisting that despite their differences, she was a good mother.

"Throughout the years, we created lasting memories and above all that, beautiful boys. Lexi and Kai are the best gifts you’ve given me and even though we have our differences, you still remain a good mother. Happy Mother’s Day," said Frankie.

After their nasty breakup, Waititu disclosed that she was searching for love again.

The mother of two, was among the guests on NTV's Gumzo la Sato segment, hosted by Lofty Matambo and Fridah Mwaka, where they discussed trending relationship stories.

When asked about her relationship with her ex-lover during the discussion, Maureen said she never thought her marriage would not work but was ready to love again.

"I can't choose who to fall in love with, but he must have values that I am looking for in a man. I am looking to date a God-fearing man," said Waititu.

Jemutai Rubbishes Claims She Broke Up with Prof Hamo after Reunion: &quot;Si Lazima Tutembe Pamoja&quot;

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Thursday, April 25, 2024
  • Jemutai made it categorically clear that she is still together with baby daddy Professor Hamo
  • The comedienne noted that she does not have to spend every minute of her life next to Hamo to prove to Kenyans that they are still together
  • She was forced to address the issue after fans started wondering why Hamo was no longer posting her on social media and vice versa

Churchill Show comedian Jemutai has rubbished claims that all is not well in her paradise.

The witty comedian in a recent media interview confirmed to Kenyans that she is still together with baby daddy Professor Hamo.

We are good

Speaking during an exclusive interview with Milele FM, the comedian confirmed everything is well in her romantic life.

The comedienne clearly stated that she is still together with Hamo, contrary to what most of her followers have been thinking.

Jemutai was forced to clear the air after her interviewer asked her why she was no longer spotted close to Hamo or being posted by the baby daddy like it was a couple of weeks ago.

''Should I walk with him everywhere to confirm that we are together? We are not supposed to parade that we are together. I even talked to Hamo today in the morning. We are perfect at the moment and we even did a show to make things clear. Right now we want to focus on our comedy careers. Right now we are focused on raising our children." Jemutai stated.

Happy reunion

The comedienne's revelation came barely four months after she reconciled with her baby daddy Professor Hamo.

Jemutai and Hamo got back together after a back and forth battle over upkeep and stuff.

The couple, however, ironed out their issues and actually proved to Kenyans that all was well in their paradise.

The comedians even bought land together and started the construction of their family home.

News about the latest development was first made public by their contractor Kathy Andrews through her Instagram page.

In a post seen by TUKO.co.ke, Kathy shared a photo of herself together with Hamo and Jemutai on their piece of land and disclosed they had done the groundbreaking for their upcoming house.

Kathy's announcement caught the attention of many social media users who camped on the comments section of her post to congratulate the couple on their milestone.

The groundbreaking came barely two weeks after TUKO.co.ke reported how Hamo and Jemutai were elated after buying their first piece of land together.

TUKO.co.ke earlier reported how the mother of two was gifted a piece of land by her husband, Prof Hamo.

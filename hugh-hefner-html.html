Hugh Hefner - Trivia, Family, Bio

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Wednesday, February 28, 2024

Magazine publisher, First Amendment advocate, and founder of Playboy Enterprises.

Before Fame

He worked as a reporter for the United States Army's military newspaper in the late 1940s.

Trivia

He earned a degree in psychology with a double minor in art and creative writing in less than three years.

Family Life

He was married to Mildred Williams from 1949 to 1959. In 1989, he married playmate of the year Kimberley Conrad. He married 26-year-old Crystal Harris on December 31, 2012, when he was 86 years old. His oldest son is named David Hefner.

Associated With

His magazine became famous for its centerfolds that featured the likes of Kim Kardashian.

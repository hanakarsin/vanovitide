Pamela Anderson Latest News and Updates

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, June 13, 2024

Canadian-American actor Pamela Anderson is probably best known for her starring role on Baywatch in the '90s. She has also worked for years as a glamour model in Playboy. She has two sons named Brandon and Dylan, whom she shares with her ex-husband Tommy Lee. The couple's volatile relationship — including the theft and distribution of their infamous sex tape — was the subject of Hulu's biographical drama miniseries Pam & Tommy.

Full Name: Pamela Denise Anderson

Birthday: July 1, 1967

Relationship Status: Currently single. Previously married to Dan Hayhurst, Jon Peters, Rick Salomon, Kid Rock, and Tommy Lee.

Instagram: @pamelaanderson

Twitter: @pamelaanderson

Net Worth: $20 million

Fun Fact: Pamela Anderson was "discovered" after appearing on the Jumbotron at a BC Lions Canadian Football League game.

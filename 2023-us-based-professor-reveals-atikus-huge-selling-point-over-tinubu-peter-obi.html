US-Based Professor Reveals Atiku's Huge Selling Point Over Tinubu, Peter Obi

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

A United States-based professor of Medicine, Emeka Umerah, has claimed that the involvement of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), presidential candidate, Atiku Abubakar, in preventing former President, Olusegun Obasanjo, from pursuing his alleged third term in office in 2007 will be a major selling point for him, in the 2023 elections.

Umerah made this known in Abuja, on Saturday, while delivering a keynote address at the public declaration of support for the presidential candidate of the PDP by the Diamond Ladies in Politics.

Umerah maintained that Atiku’s intervention saved Nigeria from returning to the era of the dark days of military dictatorship and tyrannical rule.

Recalling Atiku’s successful efforts to revive the economy under Obasanjo’s administration, the US-based professor urged Nigerians to support the PDP candidate in order to address the nation’s economic problems and deteriorating security threats.

Message To Aggrieved PDP Members

Umerah also enjoined aggrieved stalwarts of the PDP, to put their differences aside and work together to ensure the defeat of the All Progressives Congress (APC), at the presidential poll.

Atiku More Experienced Than, Tinubu, Obi, Others

Former Kogi Central lawmaker, Senator Dino Melaye has stated that Atiku, is healthier, fitter and more experienced out of all 2023 presidential candidates.

Melaye stated this during the public declaration for Atiku and induction ceremony by the Diamond Ladies in Politics held Saturday evening in Abuja, the nation’s capital.

He argued that there is no basis for comparing Atiku with the presidential candidate of the ruling All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

Melaye maintained that the supporters of the PDP Presidential candidate are real and organic, adding that Atiku is a billionaire with an enterprise.

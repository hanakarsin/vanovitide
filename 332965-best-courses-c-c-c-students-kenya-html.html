Best courses for C+, C or C minus students in 2024 in Kenya

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

Having a C plain, C minus, and sometimes C+ means that you miss out on a spot to undertake an excellent degree course in one of the best higher institutions in Kenya. However, this is not the end of your education road as there are some competitive courses for C+, C, or C minus students. The programs are guaranteed to lead to a successful career, and if need be, you can always upgrade to the highest level of education you wish.

Courses for C+, C or C minus students

Which course can I do with a C, C+ or C minus in 2024? Here is a list of the best programmes for each of the grades as well as the requirements and the institutions to join.

C minus courses in Kenya

With a C minus aggregate, you can choose between pursuing a certificate or a diploma. Fortunately for you, there are some highest paying diploma courses programmes that accept students with this grade as listed below.

1. Tourism Management

Well, do you fancy tourism? If yes, this is one of the best C minus courses you can pursue in the country. Best offered at Kenyatta University, the programme remains to be the most marketable diploma in the country.

As long as we continue to have tourist attraction sites and peace across the country, you will secure an excellent-paying job after graduation.

2. Purchasing and supplies

Purchasing and Supplies is an excellent programme in Kenya as it is one of the fastest-growing professions. Professionals in this field earn a good amount of salary per month while working for some of the biggest companies in the country.

The requirements are as follows:

  • Aggregate C-
  • D plus in English and Maths or Accounting, Business or commerce or Economics
  • Duration of 24 months

3. Web and Graphic Design

With this diploma, you can employ yourself and sometimes be outsourced by some of the biggest companies such as Safaricom and Airtel. More and more businesses are opting to improve their online presence hence the demand for technicians in this field.

The requirements are as follows:

  • C minus Grade
  • C- in English and Kiswahili or any other two subjects
  • Computer Studies is a mandatory

The corporate world will always need financial experts and accountants. It is, therefore, no wonder that this is one of the most marketable diploma courses in the current job market.

5. Human Resource Management

Human resource experts are the key decision-makers in any company or organisation. Therefore, not even one of them can survive without the services of these professionals. It makes the diploma one of the most competitive in the country.

Even though many people are currently pursuing this diploma, if you are lucky enough, you could secure employment with NGOs such as World Vision and AMREF. C courses in Kenya

C plain students have a wide range of programmes to choose from. However, the best of all the C plain courses in Kenya include the following:

1. Nutrition and Health

If you love medicine programmes, then this is one of the best programs you can do with a C-. Many private and government institutions offer this diploma so you won’t have trouble finding a suitable one for you. Here, you will gain knowledge of public health and nutrition.

Grade C in either any two subjects in the following categories:

2. Hospitality Management

Hospitality Management is one of the best courses to do with a C plain. You will acquire managerial and educational tools necessary for managing high-end hotels, restaurants and other places where hospitality is essential to a business. Experienced experts in this field earn lots of money monthly.

The requirements are as follows:

  • Category A: Biology, English/Kiswahili, Chemistry.
  • Category B: Home Science, Biological Sciences, Physical Sciences, Mathematics, English/Kiswahili.
  • Alternatively, you can pursue this course with a C minus and a certificate from a recognised institution in the same or any other related programme.

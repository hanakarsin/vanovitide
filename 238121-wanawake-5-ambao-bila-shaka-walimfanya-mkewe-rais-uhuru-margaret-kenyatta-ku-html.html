Wanawake 5 ambao bila shaka walimfanya mkewe Rais Uhuru, Margaret Kenyatta, kuingiwa na WIVU (picha)

- Uhuru hupasua kicheko kirahisi kinyume na mkewe Margaret, ambaye huona soni na hujaribu kuepuka kamera

- Wawili hao wamekuwa na muda wao hadharani lakini leo, tunaangazia wanawake wengine waliomfanya atabasamu vilevile

5.

Habari Nyingine: Msanii Size 8 ACHAPISHA picha ya dadake mtandaoni, utapenda

4.

Habari Nyingine: Mcheshi wa Churchill Show aliyedhaniwa KUFARIKI ajitokeza

Tonto Dikeh Reacts After FCDA Demolishes Ex Lover Kpokpogri's Mansion

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Friday, May 10, 2024

Nollywood actress, Tonto Dikeh has reacted to the demolition of her former lover, Prince Kpokpogri’s properties by the FCDA (Federal Capital Development Authority)

Naija News reports that the aspiring politician via his Insta story on Saturday narrates how some officers of FCDA stormed his property in the wee hours of the morning without notice and began to destroy his property.

He added that he had children and visitors in his residence when the sad occurrence happened.

“I have children and visitors inside the house and these people just came and started demolishing without any reason”, he cried out.

Reacting to the development, the actress and mother of one took to her Instagram saying that the government should be allowed to do their work as it should be.

She also said that her ex-lover who wanted to see the end of her by all means must pay for all his wrongdoings towards her.

She added that even though he wanted to kill her, she will not gloat about his downfall.

In her own words “Person wey put hand for person downfall no Dey come Instagram come gloat.
And if by mistake my hand enter too, I’ll pat my back!! This man was ready to See the end of me..

“This young man sent his sister PRECIOUS TO MOCK ME WHEN MY MOTHER DIED. And to tell me my child was next, I REMEMBER TELLING HER THEIR DOWNFALL WILL BE TELEVISED…. Kpokpogri even said I killed, you all don’t know pain

“He is not an enemy He is a friend who must pay for all his wrong doings
LET THE GOVERNMENT DO THEIR WORK AS IT SHOULD BE…”

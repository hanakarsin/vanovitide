I Will Trek From Accra To Lagos If Super Eagles Win Actor, John Dumelo

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, February 8, 2024

Popular Ghanaian actor turned politician, John Dumelo, has bragged that Ghana will defeat Nigeria in the 2022 FIFA World Cup playoffs.

The 38-year-old also vowed to trek from Accra to Lagos if the Black Stars by any means lose to Nigeria’s Super Eagles in the World Cup qualifiers.

Naija News reports the Black Stars will host the first leg of the tie on Friday, March 25 before travelling to Abuja for the reverse fixture four days later.

Kick-off for Friday night’s match is 8.30pm Nigerian time and Dumelo is so confident of his countrymen’s ability to deliver well at the Kumasi stadium.

Bragging on his Instagram page, Dumelo wrote: “There’s no way Nigeria will win today’s match.

“If they do, I John Setor Dumelo will walk barefoot from Accra to Lagos tomorrow morning with my ginger on my head. Super Eagles my foot.”

In another news, Manchester United young lad who plays from the midfielder, Maximilian Oyedele, has decided he will play internationally for Poland.

The teenage player dumped Nigeria and England and committed his future to the Polish national team.

The development was disclosed in a statement on Thursday by Maciej Chorazyk, the head of foreign scouting Poland Football Association.

Naija News understands that Oyedele, a first-year scholar at Man United, is eligible to represent Poland through his mom, Nigeria through his father, and England, where he was born.

He made his international debut on Wednesday as Poland U18s played a 2-2 draw against Switzerland in a non-binding game.

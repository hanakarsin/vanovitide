Rumer Willis - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Monday, June 24, 2024

Bruce Willis and Demi Moore's daughter, who became the winner of season 20 of Dancing with the Stars. She appeared in the films The House Bunny, Striptease and The Whole Nine Yards. In 2019, she had a stint on NBC's The Masked Singer.

Before Fame

She attended the Interlochen Arts Academy, Wildwood Secondary School, and the University of Southern California.

Trivia

She had a brief stint on 90210.

Family Life

She has two younger sisters, Scout and Tallulah. In December 2022, she announced that she was expecting her first child with her boyfriend Derek Richard Thomas. They welcomed a daughter named Louetta Isley on April 18, 2023.

Associated With

Her partner on DWTS season 20 was Valentin Chmerkovskiy.

Nigerian Army Appoints New GOCs, Principal Staff Officers (Full List)

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

New General Officers Commanding, Principal Staff Officers, Commandant of tri-service institutions, and army training centres have been appointed by the Nigerian Army.

The fresh appointments made by the Chief of Army Staff, Lt. Gen. Ibrahim Attahiru were made public in a statement on Saturday, by the Director, Army Public Relations, Brig. Gen. Mohammed Yerima.

The statement titled, ‘Nigeria Army approves new postings and appointments,’ explained that the redeployment was aimed at repositioning the army for optimal performance.

According to the statement which urged the new officers to discharge their responsibilities with “absolute loyalty to the Commander-in-Chief and in defence of the Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria,” all the appointments and postings will take effect from March 10, 2021.

Meanwhile, the Nigerian Army has denied claims that four soldiers were killed on Thursday when bandits attacked a military base in Katsina State.

The Army in an official statement released on Saturday by the Director, Army Public Relations, Brigadier-General Mohammed Yerima confirmed the clash between bandits and soldiers in Katsina on Thursday, but denied the killing of four soldiers during the clash.

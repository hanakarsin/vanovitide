Comedian Bovi and wife celebrate 11th wedding anniversary with lovely photos

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Friday, July 12, 2024

- Nigerian comedian, Bovi Ugboma and his wife, Kris Asimonye, recently took to social media to celebrate their union

- The couple celebrated their 11th wedding anniversary on September 19, 2020

- To mark the big day, lovely family photos were shared on the couple’s individual social media pages, accompanied with sweet captions

Top Nigerian comedian, Bovi Ugboma and his wife, Kris Asimonye, recently took to social media to give fans couple goals.

The duo who got married 11 years ago celebrated their wedding anniversary in a big way for all their fans to enjoy.

Being a celebrity and staying married has proven to be quite a task going by the number of relationships which seemed like goals but later hit the rocks.

Despite that, there are some celebrity relationships that give fans hope that love still exists in the entertainment industry.

To celebrate the occasion of their 11th wedding anniversary, Bovi and Kris took to their individual social media pages to share loved up photos of themselves. The couple accompanied these snaps with sweet captions.

Bovi who seemed short of words thanked his wife, Kris, for the music. According to him, their marriage had been just that, music.

Kris on the other hand was more expressive as she wrote on how she was blessed to be married to the father of her three children. According to her, he was her goldmine and the wind beneath her wings.

The lucky woman said that every day in her union, she rejoices to be married to Bovi and that he was completely undisturbed by her choice to keep her maiden name and was also unmoved by conventional ways.

Kris described Bovi as her feminist man and said that he is her king.

Excerpts from her caption reads: “Oh how blessed I am to be married to this goldmine of mine!

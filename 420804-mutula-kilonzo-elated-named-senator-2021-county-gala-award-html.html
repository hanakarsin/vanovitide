Mutula Kilonzo Elated after Being Named Best Senator in 2021 County Gala Awards

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Sunday, February 4, 2024
  • 2021 County Gala Awards ranked Machakos governor Alfred Mutua as the best performing county boss
  • Murang'a county's Sabina Chege was ranked the best performing woman representative
  • Principal Secretary in the Ministry of Interior Karanja Kibicho was named as the best performing PS

Makueni county's Mutula Kilonzo Junior is elated after being named the best senator in the 2021 County Gala Awards (CGA).

The outspoken lawmaker took to Facebook on Saturday, July 31, to express his delight, quoting Psalm 23:6.

CGA is a business and marketing company that rates leaders' performance across the country and rewards efforts by counties in their quest to implement devolution.

In this year's awards, a survey was conducted between January 1 to February 30 and results were announced in June.

The Khalid Njiraini led company ranked Machakos governor Alfred Mutua as the best performing county boss.

Murang'a county's Sabina Chege was named the best performing woman representative, while Rarieda lawmaker Otiende Amollo was ranked as the best performing MP.

Nairobi senator Johnson Sakaja received the Political Leadership Award.

Caroline Karugu of Nyeri county was awarded the best deputy governor.

Nakuru's Governor Lee Kinyanjui leads was named the best performing county.

Best performers in ministries

The award named Ambassador Amina Mohammed as the best performing Cabinet Secretary (CS). Amina heads the Ministry of Sports, Heritage and Culture.

Principal Secretary, State Department of Interior and Citizen Services in the Ministry of Interior Karanja Kibicho was named the best performing PS.

Further, CGA also recognised Ministry of Health Chief Administrative Secretary, Mercy Mwangangi. She was instrumental after the emergence of COVID-19 in Kenya in March 2020.

Mizani Africa

Elsewhere, Tharaka Nithi Governor Muthomi Njuki was ranked the best performing governor in the country in a 2021 performance index done by Mizani Africa.

According to Mizani Africa, Governor Muthomi was followed by Kirinyaga's Anne Waiguru, while Nandi's Stephen Sang' clinched the third position.

Other leaders who were also awarded for their best performance are Embakasi East MP Babu Owino.

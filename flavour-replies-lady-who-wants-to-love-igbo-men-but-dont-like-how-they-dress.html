Flavour Replies Lady Who Wants To Love Igbo Men But Don't Like How They Dress

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, February 29, 2024

Nigerian singer, Flavour has replied a lady fan who expressed her fears about falling in love with Igbo men.

This comes after the singer via his Twitter handle shared a video of himself donning a sleeveless top and pants.

In reaction to Flavour’s sense of fashion, the female fan, via his comment section, expressed her worries about the sense of style of Igbo men.

According to her, she desires to be with an Igbo man, however, she is worried by their unique dress sense.

She wrote: “I want to fall in love with an Igbo man but I’m so scared that one day he can just come out of the room dressed like this and he’d be so serious.”

In response to the lady’s comment, Flavour assured her that she would be fine.

“You’ll be fine,” the singer wrote.

Ike Onyema Confesses To Pimping BBNaija Girls To Big Boys

Barely 24 hours after the Big Brother Naija ‘All-Stars’ premiered, one of the housemates, Ike Onyema, recently confessed to pimping his female colleagues to big boys.

Naija News reports that the reality TV star corroborated a claim his colleague, Whitemoney, had earlier made this year.

Whitemoney, during an interview with media personality, Nedu, claimed that BBNaija girls often plead with him to link them up with his rich friends.

However, Ike, while discussing with some of the male housemates, said he pimps BBNaija girls to his friends.

Ike stressed that he makes money when he organises parties for his friends, who often request Big Brother Naija girls.

He said, “How I make the most money is when I organize parties for big boys and these boys call me to arrange big Brother babes for them. Venita knows how many times I have called her. No be me owe the girls, I just dey do friend, friend things with them”

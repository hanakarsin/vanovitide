Iron Lady: Minicheps Photoshops Herself Wearing Sufuria, Forks in Hilarious Harmonize Mimic

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Wednesday, February 28, 2024

- Minicheps joined comedians in making fun of Harmonize's cutlery inspired outfit

- The young artist shared a hilarious photo of her mini-self donning a sufuria, spoon and forks

- The photo left many in stitches and she was lauded for the amazing creativity

Famous photographer Minicheps, born Catherine Jepkemboi, has done it again with her impressive photoshop art, where she looks like the world's tiniest person.

The artist joined the celebrity bandwagon that has been mimicking Bongo star Harmonize's recent outfit in which he wore nearly a household's full set of cutlery.

In a hilarious photo shared on social media, Minicheps took the mimic to another level as she appeared to be donning a sufuria as a skirt, plus forks and spoons, which formed her top.

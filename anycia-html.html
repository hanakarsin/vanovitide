Anycia - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Rapper, musician, and entertainer who is known for songs like "BRB" and "ANYCIA." In November of 2023, she released a six-track EP titled EXTRA. Her music often consists of her signature mellow vocals over varied hip-hop beats. 

Before Fame

It was in the summer of 2022 when she made the official decision to pursue a music career. In September 2022, she released her debut single "KIMORA LEE." 

Trivia

In September of 2023, she performed at Slangfest in Atlanta. In October of 2023, she went on tour with fellow rapper Veeze. 

Family Life

She was born in the United States. 

Associated With

In October of 2023, she posted a photo slideshow on her anyciaaaaaa Instagram account, which featured a picture of herself with New York rapper Jim Jones.

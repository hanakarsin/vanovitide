Ludacris - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Grammy Award-winning rapper who hit the charts in 2000 with the album Back for the First Time. He has had a second career as an actor and has appeared in several films in the Fast and Furious series.

Before Fame

He began rapping in Atlanta, Georgia at the age of nine and joined an amateur rap group at age twelve.

Trivia

His real name is Christopher Brian Bridges; he co-founded the record label Disturbing tha Peace and released Battle of the Sexes in 2010.

Family Life

He was born to Roberta Shields and Wayne Brian Bridges. He married Eudoxie Mbouguiengue in 2014. He has four daughters named Karma, Cai, Cadence and Chance.

Associated With

He collaborated with Usher and Lil Jon on the hit 2004 song "Yeah!"

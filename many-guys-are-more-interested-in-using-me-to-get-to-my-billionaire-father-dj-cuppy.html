Many Guys Are More Interested In Using Me To Get To My Billionaire Father

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Friday, March 22, 2024

Popular Nigerian disc Jockey, Florence Ifeoluwa Otedola, popularly known as DJ Cuppy has opened up on her relationship woes.

The billionaire daughter in a recent interview said that most men seem to be more interested in her father than her.

She explained that she wants a man who would love her for who she is and not mind she is the daughter of the famous Nigerian billionaire, Femi Otedola.

She said that finding a man who loves her has now become difficult because of her billionaire father.

She revealed how a guy she was dating kept asking when he would meet her father.

The 29-year-old producer said, “So many guys don’t want me, they want my dad. I dated a guy, and he was like “when is he going to meet my dad.”

“I want someone to love the shit out of me and like be happy and not my dad.” She added.

Banky W Signs Whitemoney

Former Big Brother Naija (BBNaija) reality star, Hazel Oyeze Onou, popularly known as Whitemoney has been signed into Empire Mates Entertainment, a music record label owned by Banky W.

“Now that we are in good hands, let the music flow,” WhiteMoney posted on his verified Instagram page on Tuesday to announce his latest achievement.

The entertainer also shared photos of himself Banky W on social media to celebrate the win.

Naija News understands that the 30-year-old shot into the limelight after emerging winner of the season 6 edition of Nigeria’s popular reality show.

Whitemoney also hinted in his Instagram post that he would be dropping two singles in September.

Corinna Kopf - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Thursday, March 28, 2024

Social media personality who is particularly known for her Instagram page. She has gained her following through her posts detailing her everyday life. She has also been known online as Pouty Girl. 

Before Fame

She began posting to Instagram on August 18, 2012. She was still a high school student at the time of her first post.

Trivia

She has garnered more than 6 million followers to her Instagram page. Also a Fortnite gamer, she signed an exclusive streaming deal with Facebook Gaming in 2019 after previously streaming her content on Twitch. 

Family Life

She grew up in Palatine, Illinois; but has family scattered throughout the midwest in Wisconsin, Iowa and Missouri. She'd later reside in Los Angeles, California. She dated Turner Tenney in 2019. She also previously dated Toddy Smith.

Associated With

She has collaborated with good friend and fellow YouTuber Liza Koshy.

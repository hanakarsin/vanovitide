'I Will Teach You A Life Lesson Between Having Money, Sense And Respect'

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Monday, April 15, 2024

Nigerian talent manager, Tunji Balogun, better known as Tee Billz, the ex-husband to sensational singer, Tiwa Savage, has slammed Nigerian singer, Davido, for disrespecting his family.

Naija News reports that Tee Billz made this known in a post via Instagram on Monday while threatening to teach Davido a lesson between having money, sense and respect.

According to Tiwa’s ex-husband, protecting one’s family is not just a duty but the essence of a man’s character, a testament to his love and commitment.

He claimed that Davido disrespected his family despite the love his son, Jamil and Tiwa Savage, had shown his daughter, Imade.

Tee Billz noted that Davido has been getting away with disrespect to people but would teach him a lesson because he is not Dele Momodu or Amaju, adding he would wait till the Grammy Awards is over before giving details of what transpired.

He said, “Protecting one’s family is not just a duty; it’s the essence of a man’s character, a testament to his love and commitment……… After the love Jamil, “The Mother of my Son”, and I have shown towards your daughter…… My family is the last one you will ever disrespect in Nigeria… it’s not a threat like I told Bobo!

“You’ve been getting away with disrespect DAVID ADELEKE….. I’m not Uncle Dele or Amaju. I will teach you a lesson on their behalf also……I gave you every chance but you thought it was another one! They warned you not to Fuck with me! I will teach you a life lesson about having MONEY, SENSE, and RESPECT….. I pause till after you enjoy your Grammy like I told Tunde Ednut just from the kindness of my heart!!!.”

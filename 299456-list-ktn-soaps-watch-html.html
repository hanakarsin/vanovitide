List of KTN soaps you should watch

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

From daylight to prime time, KTN soaps have been capturing a big audience since way back. No one wants to miss the juicy drama, comedy, action and above all the romance. Some people get glued to their screen that the only way you can get them off or manage to have a conversation with them is by switching off the television.

For more than five years now, Kenya Television Network (KTN) has proven to go above and beyond in becoming the best home of soap operas. Below is a list of KTN soaps that you should watch.

1. Dolce Amore

Dolce Amore is a 2016 KTN soap opera of romance and comedy. The story revolves around a young, handsome man by the name Tenten who falls deeply in love with Serena, an Italian woman who has run away from home. Serena a young, gorgeous lady is adopted by one wealthy family from Italy. The rich family secretly arranges a marriage for her. Upon making this discovery, Serena decides to run away and goes to the Philippines. While in the Philippines, the unexpected happens; she meets and finds herself in love with a young, hardworking but poor boy from a small town called Manila. The two young individuals do not let their background and geographical differences hinder what they honestly feel about each other.

2. Until We Meet Again

The 2016 soap opera that premiered in 2016 stars Bea Binene as Ana Isabella Medrano. Odessa is strongly obsessed with Larry and will do anything to have him by her side. Odessa plans on destroying Larry’s relationship with Evelyn by kidnapping their daughter one and only daughter Ana. A blame game arises between Larry and Evelyn as she blames Larry for the disappearance of their daughter. It creates a rift between them, and they end up separated as was Odessa’s plan. Odessa locks up the baby in an abandoned warehouse in the company of a dog which makes her develop all the habits and behaviours of that animal. Eventually, Ana manages to escape from the warehouse and meets Calvin who rescues him, and later on, they fall in love.

3. Doble Kara

Doble Kara has been one of the majority's favourites and the fact that it comes in three different seasons only makes things spicy. The storyline of this KTN soap revolves around two identical twin sisters Sara and Kara who have been brought up in a humble yet happy family. Their strong sisterhood bond is brought to test by jealousy, the quest of self-identity and exposure to revenge by Lucille. In the second book, Kara marries Sebastian Acosta marking the beginning of a new life. Sara, on the other hand, begins a new life in by moving to Japan.

4. The Promise

The Promise is a soap that kept many people glued to their screens for a very long time. Some of the main character cast in this soap include; Kristine Hermosa, Jericho Rosales, Eula Valdez, and Jean Garcia. Just like all the other soap operas, The Promise entails forbidden love. There is a romantic relationship between Eduardo and Amor. However, Dona, Eduardo’s mother forbids this romantic relationship with ulterior motives. Dona wants Eduardo to marry another woman, Claudia, due to some hidden political agendas beneficial to her. The romance and the drama surrounding the entire soap is something you don't want to miss for whatever reason.

5. Forever More

This love story revolves around a rebellious and reckless guy by the name Xander and a diligent and aggressive lady by the name Agnes. One day Xander goes on an adventurous trip with a parachute. He goes on this venture while intoxicated and as expected the aftermath is not so good. He crashes his parachute into a strawberry truck from Trinidad. In sought of making him pay for his mistakes and learn his lessons, his parents choose the hard way for him. He is forced to work on the strawberry farm under the close supervision of Agnes; the Queen of Strawberry Jam together with the other farmers. It is during this punishment encounter there emerges an extraordinary relationship between the two.

6. Someone to Watch Over Me

'Someone to Watch Over Me’ is a KTN soap opera that narrates the love story of TJ Chavez and Joanna but later the unexpected happens. TJ Chavez a young, full of life and outgoing guy marries the love of his life, Joanna. Early into the marriage TJ Chavez is diagnosed with the early onset of Alzheimer's disease and this turns their lives upside down. Joanna would do everything for his love and husband; for instance, she allows TJ's ex, Irene both into their home and lives all for his husband. TJ shifts his affection and attention to Irene while Joanna acts as the nurse taking care of all his needs. How far and long can Joanna take this treatment? Will TJ go on pouring love to Irene or will he remember that Joana is his wife?

7. Wild Flower

There is nothing as thrilling and captivating as a soap opera that combines its romance with instances of drama, crime, and action. Wildflower is one such kind TV series of between 2017-2018 starring Tirso Cruz III, Aiko Melendez, and Maja Salvador. The soap opera is a narration about one fine-looking and smart heiress by the name Ivy Aguas. Ivy Aguas is on a revenge mission against the Ardiente family which is responsible for the death of her parents. The story brings out the themes of revenge as seen in Ivy's mission, oppression of Ivy and her family which follows the loss of her parents through murder. In simple terms, vengeance has on no occasion been this wild especially upon being undertaken by such a lovely flower.

8. Walang Hanggan- (My Eternal)

My Eternal is a soap opera full of drama, action and a little bit of crime starring Coco Martin, Julia Montes, and Dawn Zulueta. The story takes place in the fictitious province of Olivarez where two sisters, Virginia and Margaret are competing for the affection of one man; Joseph Montenegro. Virginia makes the sacrifice of losing everything so that Joseph and her sister can enjoy a better life.

10. Adventure of Tintin

Tintin is an unquenchably curious young reporter in the company of Snowy; his loyal dog notices a classic ship carrying a volatile secret. It draws Tintin into the mystery of the past centuries and cannot escape from the line of sight of Ivan Ivanovitch. Ivan is a dreadful villain who accuses Tintin of stealing a precious gem that he believes it belonged to Red Rackham a devious pirate.

11. Losing Heaven

Losing Heaven is an electrifying love story full of obsession and jealousy. Two high school best friends Ellery and Bridgette come from two different social statuses. One day, the two girls become instantaneous ‘sisters’ as they discover themselves living with the same set of parents and the same house. It occurs after the demise of Bridgette’s parents; hence Ellery’s father resolves to adopt her.

Bridgette meets and immediately fall in love with the Jonas, a young and handsome bachelor. Unfortunately, Jonas is deeply in love with Ellery. Jonas proposes to Ellery, and she says yes. Although Bridgette paves the way to this romantic relationship, she carries a strong resentment against her best friend and ‘sister'.

12. I Will Never Say Goodbye

I Will Never Say Goodbye is another must watch Kenya meets the Philippines KTN soap opera. The storyline revolves around torn lovers and romance between Waldo; an upright and hardworking guy from a happy family and Aryann. A man tries to rape Waldo’s lover Aryann, and in defence of Aryann, Waldo kills the man. He is then sent to jail for murder, but he is later on released as the killing was a self-defence act. Waldo gets lost in the sea, and everyone including Aryann concludes that he is dead. Aryann has no choice but to move on, where she meets Tristan, and they fall in love. Later on, Waldo shows up and tries to fight for Aryann.

These are some of the best KTN soaps you should watch. Go ahead and enjoy the thrilling storyline narrated by the soaps.

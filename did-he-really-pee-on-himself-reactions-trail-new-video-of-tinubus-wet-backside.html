Did He Really Pee On Himself? Reactions Trail Disturbing New Video Of Tinubu's Wet Backside

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Friday, March 15, 2024

Nigerians on social media have reacted to a viral video of the leader of the All Progressive Congress, Bola Tinubu soaked behind in Ijebu Ode.

Naija News reports that presidential aspirant, Bola Tinubu’s dress was soaked behind when he stood up to make a statement on Saturday at the palace of Awujale of Ijebuland in Ijebu Ode.

Reacting to the development, some social media users urged the APC leader to focus on his health rather than on the presidency.

They blamed the people in the room cheering him despite the embarrassing state the APC leader was visibly in.

Amongst people who reacted was Reno Omokri, who insisted that there’s nothing wrong with the APC leader peeing on himself as it is old age-related issue.

I also pray it does not happen to me when I am advanced in age. I mean no disrespect, but this man should focus on his health!

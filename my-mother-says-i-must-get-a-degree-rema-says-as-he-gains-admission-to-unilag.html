'My Mother Says I Must Get A Degree'

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Popular Nigerian Singer, Divine Ikubor with the stage name Rema has announced his admission into the University of Lagos (UNILAG).

The singer stated via Twitter that his mother had insisted that he has to get a degree despite having a successful career.

He went on to state that he has gotten admission into UNILAG in the Creative Arts department and would be resuming classes soon.

Below are some Nigerians reactions to the latest achievement coming from the singer.

Ken Yoda: “Mummy I love you…that shows you’re still in charge unlike some other yeyebrities dia parent can not control dem again…I can mention names…but today dia fame don enter gutter…GOD bless u…she knows education with fame is a plus”

Anonymous: “He’s in the right department. They mostly run the course in practical so I believe he will concentrate well and excel”

Ojvibez “I thought he got management and shit?? They can’t advise him that he’s not supposed to disclose his department or news of admission?? Guy cause of Rema, girls go apply for the course alone, also he won’t be able to focus cause security guards go de follow am waka up and down, thirdly lecturers will be tough on him, cause they will think he might wana use his fame to get good grades. Best option was to study abroad, where people there respect themselves and he be able to study well . I can mark with money say this guy nor ohh fit go class, except he’s given private class sessions.”

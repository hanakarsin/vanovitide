Aisha Buhari Boards Kaduna-Abuja Train (Photos)

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Friday, June 7, 2024

The First Lady of Nigeria, Aisha Buhari on Thursday was spotted on the Abuja-Kaduna train.

Recall that the first lady visited Kano State where he charged the Governor of the state, Abdullahi Ganduje, to ensure justice for five-year-old, Hanifa Abubakar who was kidnapped and murdered by her teacher.

The media team of the first lady had first shared a video of her boarding a flight from Kano to Kaduna, after which pictures of her trip to Abuja by train were released.

Aisha who sat next to her younger brother, Mahmud Ahmad, also had her aides on board.

Military men and operatives of the Department of State Services (DSS) were seen behind the compartment of the train where she sat.

Wike, Other Ministers-designate Document Ahead Of Inauguration (Photos)

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Thursday, April 4, 2024

Ministers-designate for the administration of President Bola Tinubu have continued their documentation exercise ahead of the swearing-in scheduled for Monday, 21st August 2023.

Naija News earlier reported the commencement of the exercise on Saturday.

Some other Minister-designates including former Rivers State Governor, Nyesom Wike have also gone through the documentation process after the earlier report by this platform.

The documentation took place at the Office Of The Secretary To The Government Of The Federation in Abuja.

‘How Will They Perform In Office’ – President Tinubu Ministerial Portfolio Causes Upset, Shock

Political pundits and other critical stakeholders have been left in shock following the assigning of portfolios for ministers-designate by President Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

Naija News gathered that many people were left in shock after Tinubu assigned portfolios to individuals who many believe are not experts in that field.

The appointment of former Rivers State Nyesom Wike, for instance, altered the earlier permutation that Abubakar Kyari from Borno State would be named Minister of the Federal Capital Territory (FCT).

Wike’s appointment makes it the first time since 1999, and in over 40 years, a Southern Christian would be named substantive FCT minister.

Umahi’s appointment similarly came to many as a surprise. Since 1999, no South Easterner has served as Minister of Works. Only South-South, North-West, and South-West have served as Works ministers.

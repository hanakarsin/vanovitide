'All American' Fans, Rejoice! Season 5 Netflix Release Date Revealed

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Wednesday, March 13, 2024

When will 'All American' Season 5 be on Netflix? 'All American' Season 5 released on the CW months before it hit Netflix. So, when can you stream it?

Fans of the CW’s football drama All American have been riding with the show from the beginning. The series, which is based on the life of former NFL star Spencer Paysinger, showcases the personal and athletic life of Spencer (Daniel Ezra) and his family and loved ones.

Article continues below advertisement

After the Season 5 finale aired on the CW on May 15, 2023, many viewers were still left with one question in mind: When will All American Season 5 be on Netflix? After all, it’s a great way to binge watch the series with ease. Here’s everything that we know.

Article continues below advertisement

When does 'All American' Season 5 release on Netflix?

Months after All American Season 5 aired on the CW, fans were eager to know when it would drop on Netflix. Well, it's time to butter up that popcorn, as your next fix of football drama is available now: All American Season 5 dropped on Netflix on May 23, 2023.

Article continues below advertisement

Unlike some of the first few seasons, Season 5 of All American has 20 episodes. Seasons 1 and 2 both have 16 episodes, with Season 3 having 19 and Season 4 having 20.

Since All American showrunners upped the episode count as seasons passed, it wasn't surprising to find out that All American Season 5 has 20 episodes.

Article continues below advertisement

When did Season 5 of 'All American' first come out?

Although it wasn't until May 23, 2023, that All American made its debut on Netflix, the show's fifth season actually first aired on the CW on Oct. 10, 2022. Episodes aired Mondays at 8 p.m. EST.

Will there be an ‘All American’ Season 6?

As per Variety, All American was renewed for sixth season. So, if you weren't ready to part ways with Taye Diggs or Monet Mazur, you won't have to just yet. While we don't know when Season 6 of All American will release, it could potentially happen in October 2023, possibly following the same schedule producers used for Seasons 4 and 5.

Article continues below advertisement

Although the show will make a comeback for a sixth season, many fans have voiced their opinions about All American being on their last legs in terms of storyline purposes. Many people believe that once the storyline of Olivia exposing Spencer and Jordan’s coach wraps, there’s not much to dig into on the show.

Despite this, the show seems to be captivating its audience (and there's still more story to tell). The show was rated a 92 percent by Rotten Tomatoes, and overall, viewers love the show — even those who aren't die-hard football fans.

All episodes of Season 5 of All American can now be streamed on Netflix. And stay tuned for updates on the Season 6 release date.
